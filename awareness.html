General Safety Tips

Plan Your Route

Always plan your route in advance and share it with a trusted contact. Avoid shortcuts through isolated areas.

Keep Phone Charged

Ensure your phone is fully charged before leaving. Carry a power bank for longer journeys.

Stay in Public View

Walk in well-lit, populated areas. Avoid wearing headphones at high volume in unfamiliar areas.

Transportation Safety

Verify cab details before boarding. Share ride details with someone. Sit in the back seat.

Carry Essentials

Carry some cash, ID, and emergency contact numbers separately from your wallet.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, leave immediately. Go to the nearest safe public place.
