How SOS Works

  • Emergency SOS: Triggers loud alarm, shares your location with emergency contacts, and highlights emergency numbers
  • Silent SOS Mode: Discreetly copies your location to clipboard without any sound or visible alerts
  • Your current location will be automatically detected and shared
  • Emergency contacts will receive your location and a distress message
