Safety Resources

Helplines and guidance for before, during, and after incidents

After-Incident Checklist

Follow these steps if you've experienced or witnessed a safety incident.

1

Ensure Immediate Safety

Move to a safe location. If injured, call ambulance (102) immediately.

2

Contact Emergency Services

Call police (100) or women's helpline (1091). Provide clear location details.

3

Preserve Evidence

Do not delete messages, photos, or any evidence. Take screenshots if needed.

4

Contact Trusted Person

Reach out to a family member, friend, or colleague you trust.

5

Seek Medical Attention

Even if injuries seem minor, get medical examination and document injuries.

6

File Official Report

File FIR at nearest police station. Take a copy of the report for your records.

7

Access Support Services

Contact counseling services and legal aid organizations for support.

8

Self-Care

Practice self-care, talk to supportive people, and consider professional counseling.
